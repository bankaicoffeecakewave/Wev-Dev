Welcome to NIT Rourkela

NIT Rourkela is one of the premier national level institutions for technical education in the country and is funded by the Government of India. Government of India has elevated the Regional Engineering College, Rourkela to a deemed university under the name of National Institute of Technology, Rourkela.

National Institute of Technology, Rourkela (NIT Rourkela) is a prestigious institution of higher learning for engineering, science and technology in India. It was established in 1961 as a Regional Engineering College and later upgraded to a deemed university in 2002. It is one of the 31 National Institutes of Technology in the country and is funded by the Government of India. NIT Rourkela offers undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programs in various disciplines of engineering, science, management and humanities. It also conducts research and consultancy projects in collaboration with industry and academia. NIT Rourkela has a sprawling campus of 647 acres with state-of-the-art facilities and infrastructure. It has been ranked among the top engineering institutes in India by various national and international agencies. NIT Rourkela aims to become an internationally acclaimed institution of excellence that will serve as a source of knowledge and expertise for the society.
